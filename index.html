Study Tips

  • Create a study schedule and stick to it
  • Focus on understanding concepts rather than memorizing
  • Practice with previous years' questions
  • Join study groups for discussions

Exam Information

  • Written examination with multiple choice and descriptive questions
  • Viva voce examination after passing written test
  • Minimum passing marks: 40% in each paper and 50% in aggregate
  • Held twice a year (typically in June and December)
